About Me

Who am I?

My name is Julia Truedsson and I'm 21 years old. I'm born and raised in the city Karlskrona, which is located in the south of Sweden.

During high school I studied Game Graphics, 3D, and Animation. The main focus was on 3D modelling with hard surfaces, which I then showcased in my final project by modelling a city street view. I'm currently studying New Media Design (NMD), at Jönköping University (JU). I wanted to combine creativity with technology, which led me to NMD and Jönköping.

Interest

During my free time I like to spend time at home, by watching a series or playing games with my friends. I really enjoy travelling, and often do so when there is a concert I would like to attend.
